Our Mission
Innovatech Solutions is dedicated to pioneering advancements in artificial intelligence and machine learning. Our mission is to build sustainable, ethical, and highly efficient AI-powered tools that solve real-world problems for businesses and consumers alike. We believe in the power of technology to augment human potential.

Human Resources Policies
Vacation Policy
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 paid vacation days per year. This is in addition to national holidays. Vacation days are accrued on a monthly basis. New employees can start taking vacation after completing their initial 90-day probationary period. Unused vacation days, up to a maximum of 5, can be rolled over to the next calendar year.

Work-From-Home (WFH) Policy
Innovatech Solutions operates on a hybrid model. Employees are expected to be in the office on Tuesdays and Thursdays for collaborative work. The remaining three days (Monday, Wednesday, Friday) are optional for remote work. All remote work must be conducted from within the country. Any exceptions must be approved by your direct manager.

IT and Technical Support
Getting Help
If you are experiencing technical difficulties with your computer or software, please contact the IT Help Desk immediately. You can reach them by emailing helpdesk@innovatech.example.com or by calling extension #404. For urgent issues, please state the urgency in your subject line or initial call.

Software Access
To request access to new software, you must file a ticket through the IT portal. All software requests require manager approval and are subject to licensing availability. Please allow 2-3 business days for the request to be processed.
